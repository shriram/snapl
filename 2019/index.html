SNAPL 2019

THE 3RD SUMMIT ON
ADVANCES IN PROGRAMMING LANGUAGES

The Summit oN Advances in Programming Languages (SNAPL) is a biennial venue for discussions about programming languages. It has run twice before: 2015 and 2017. The 2019 proceedings are online.

SNAPL focuses on experience-based insight, innovation, and visionary ideas spanning from foundations to applications of programming languages. We welcome perspectives from both industry and academia.

SNAPL also emphasizes discussion. The format follows the process described here. A good SNAPL contribution is thus equal part insightful paper and engaging talk. There will also be generous breaks for attendees to mingle and talk.

SNAPL 2019 took place in the Brown University Computer Science Department, located in Providence, RI, USA, on the US East Coast [Location].

Important dates:

Submission deadline: Feb 14, 2019 (Anywhere On Earth) [FIRM]
Decisions announced: Mar 15, 2019
Final versions due: Apr 15, 2019
Conference: May 16-17, 2019
