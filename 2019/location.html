3 Location

The event will be held in the computer science department of Brown University in Providence, RI, USA.

Providence is on the US East Coast, within an hour of Boston and three hours of New York. It is accessible entirely by reasonably-priced public transit (indeed, for most people, renting a car may be more burden than it’s worth).
